About Harnoor Kaur

I’m Harnoor Kaur, a data-driven Business Analyst based in Canada. I specialize in delivering actionable insights that empower businesses to make informed decisions. With a strong foundation in data analysis, project management, and stakeholder communication, I help organizations navigate their most pressing challenges with confidence.

My professional journey began with a curiosity for how businesses grow. That curiosity led me to explore the world of analytics and business intelligence. I’ve since developed expertise in tools like Power BI, SQL, Excel, Tableau, and Python to uncover trends, identify gaps, and streamline operations.

I believe great analysis goes beyond numbers. It's about asking the right questions, aligning with strategic goals, and delivering solutions that create long-term value. Whether it's mapping processes, building dashboards, or writing clear business cases, I bring clarity and precision to every step.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring cultures, reading non-fiction, and learning new languages. My approach is rooted in curiosity, empathy, and adaptability—traits that help me thrive in fast-paced, collaborative environments.
